As a woman, mother, IT professional, and seasoned Martial Artist, Jo has dedicated years to sharing her vast knowledge and skills with eager learners. From teaching children as young as 4 years to guiding seniors at 78, Jo has empowered countless individuals to become true Self Defenders, master their inner selves, or simply helped them to enjoy an active lifestyle.

Jo passionately believes that being a skilled Martial Artist or Self Defender requires both physical prowess and mental acuity to navigate any situation. She emphasizes the importance of developing self-awareness, situational awareness, and planning your next move.

In our tech-driven world, Jo leverages her IT expertise to provide best practices and essential knowledge to protect you and your family from cyber threats. With Jo, you're not just learning martial arts; you're gaining a comprehensive toolkit for personal and digital safety.
